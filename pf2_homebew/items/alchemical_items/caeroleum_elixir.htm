With an earthy flavour and the smell of tree bark, this elixir will accelerate your healing process and when applied directly to an infected wound will grant an item bonus to the next 3 savings throws against diseases and poisons in the next hour after applied.

Type minor; Level 1; Price 3 gp

The elixir restores 2d4 Hit Points, and the saving throw bonus is +2
